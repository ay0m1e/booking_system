Opening Hours:
We are open Monday to Saturday from 9am to 6pm.

Bookings:
Customers can book services online through our booking system.
Walk-ins are accepted but bookings are prioritised.

Cancellations:
Bookings can be cancelled through the My Bookings page.
Cancellations must be made before the appointment time.

Children:
We offer haircuts for both boys and girls.

Payments:
Payment is made in person at the salon unless online payment is enabled.
