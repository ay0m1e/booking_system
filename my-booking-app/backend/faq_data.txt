Salon Details:
- Mane Society hair studio at 12 Liverpool Street, London (near Liverpool Street Station).
- Contact: +44 7917 435 250 or hello@manesociety.com.
- Opening hours: Monday to Saturday, 9:00am–6:00pm. Closed on Sundays.
- Limited street parking nearby; plan extra time for London traffic.
- Walk-ins are welcome when chairs are free, but online bookings are prioritised.

Booking & Site Navigation:
- Book online via the Services page or the “Book Appointment” button on the homepage.
- Service cards show the duration and price; click “Book Now” to pick a date and time.
- Create an account or sign in to keep appointments in your My Bookings dashboard.
- Use My Bookings to view, reschedule, or cancel upcoming appointments.
- For quick help, you can also reach out via phone or email listed in the footer.

Cancellations & Late Arrivals:
- Cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your slot at no charge.
- Same-day cancellations or no-shows may incur a 50% service fee; repeat no-shows may require a deposit for future bookings.
- If you’re running late, please call us—arrivals 10+ minutes late may be shortened or rebooked.

Payments & Deposits:
- Pay in person at the salon (card or cash). Online payment is currently not enabled.
- Some long color or treatment services may require a deposit; we’ll let you know during booking if needed.

Services & Policies:
- We offer cuts, styling, color work, and kids’ cuts (boys and girls).
- Color services may need a patch test 48 hours in advance; we’ll contact you if required.
- Appointment slots run hourly from 09:00 to 17:00; the last booking starts at 17:00.

Extras:
- Browse Our Gallery and Reviews on the homepage to see our work and client feedback.
- Please arrive 5 minutes early to settle in—water, coffee, and tea are available in the lounge.
